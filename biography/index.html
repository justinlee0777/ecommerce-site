Biography

Alexa Yang is a soon to be graduating senior attending Coral Springs Charter School. Born and raised in Coral Springs, Florida as the second daughter of Chinese and Vietnamese immigrants, Alexa was brought up in an environment where persistence and integrity were of utmost importance.

From an early age, the notion of striving ahead and being extremely goal-oriented was ingrained in her. Upon entering Coral Springs Charter School in 6th grade, she has always pushed herself to take difficult classes and complete her work to the best of her abilities, no matter the size or importance of it. With feelings of fulfillment, Alexa is proud to say that through her hard efforts, she has diligently been able to consistently be on the “A” honor roll and become apart of the top 5% of her graduating class, through maintaining a 4.0 unweighted GPA. Not only does she value hard work and the determination to succeed academically, but she also places pride in other activities that she partakes in. Alexa is currently the elected Editor for Key Club, the Historian for International Club, and has been a Senator for SGA for 4 consecutive years. She believes that through these roles, it has enabled her to grow as someone who is dependable, creative, a team member, and most importantly, a leader.

When outside of school, (prior to COVID-19) Alexa volunteered frequently at the Coral Springs Art Museum and began to volunteer at the Broward Health Center as a way to explore a possible career path. Inspired by these experiences, she one day hopes to have a career in the science field.
